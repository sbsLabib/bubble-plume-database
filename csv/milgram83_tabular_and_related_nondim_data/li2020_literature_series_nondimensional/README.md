# Nondimensional literature-series data from Li et al. comparison plots

This folder contains cleaned nondimensional data for literature series uploaded as Excel files:

- Fannelop & Sjoen (1980), shown as FS80 in Li et al. (2020)
- Milgram & Van Houten (1982), shown as MV82 in Li et al. (2020)

## Variables

- `z_hat` = `z/D`
- `b_g_over_D` = `b_g/D`
- `W_c_over_W_s` = `W_c/W_s`
- `Q_over_D2_W_s` = `Q/(D^2 W_s)`
- `M_over_D2_W_s2` = `M/(D^2 W_s^2)`
