Paper synopsis

Milgram (1983) is a foundational study on the mean-flow behavior of round bubble plumes. The paper reviews previous experimental studies, identifies reliable datasets, introduces larger-scale measurements, and combines these results with integral plume theory. The selected public download for this repository preserves paper-level data and provenance in a single clean file.

Source publication

Milgram, J. H. (1983). Mean flow in round bubble plumes. Journal of Fluid Mechanics, 133, 345–376.

https://doi.org/10.1017/S0022112083001950
