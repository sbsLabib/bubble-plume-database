Paper synopsis

Li et al. (2020) experimentally investigated how bubble size and bubble population modify the integral behavior of bubble plumes in quiescent, unstratified water. The public data file supports nondimensional comparison plots for plume half-width, centerline velocity, liquid volume flux, and momentum flux.

Source publication

Li, G., Wang, B., Wu, H., & DiMarco, S. F. (2020). Impact of bubble size on the integral characteristics of bubble plumes in quiescent and unstratified water. International Journal of Multiphase Flow, 125, 103230.

https://doi.org/10.1016/j.ijmultiphaseflow.2020.103230

Data notes

  • The Li et al. 2020 CSV uses a wide paired-series format for nondimensional comparison plots.
  • Plot blocks are identified using fields such as plot_id, plot_title, source_figure, x_variable, and y_variable.
  • Paired columns include series such as li2020_air_stone_x/li2020_air_stone_y, li2020_single_orifice_x/li2020_single_orifice_y, milgram83_x/milgram83_y, fs80_x/fs80_y, and mv82_x/mv82_y.
  • Older split CSV files are source/development files and are not linked as public downloads.
