Data notes

Processing and metadata status

  • The cleaned CSV files contain nondimensionalized plotting data.
  • Rows were sorted numerically by z_hat.
  • Case labels such as AS/SO or Q1/Q2 are not included unless explicitly available in the CSV files.
  • Future metadata may add case-level labels and experimental-condition fields.
